    ABOUT DRIWAKE

    Driver Drowsiness Alert SystemS

    DRIWAKE's multifaceted approach combines driver drowsiness mitigation, collision alerts, smart speed control, real-time location tracking, and emergency response integration to significantly enhance road safety and reduce accidents caused by both harmful gases and driver fatigue within the truck cabin.

    • 01Comprehensive Emergency Response System:

      It integrates a robust emergency response system. In critical situations, the system initiates emergency calls to nearby National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) centers and pre-configured emergency contacts, ensuring swift assistance and potentially preventing accidents.

      Scroll Up

    • 02 Driver Drowsiness Mitigation:

      The system incorporates driver drowsiness detection technology, actively working to mitigate the risk of accidents caused by driver fatigue. DRIWAKE provides alerts to the driver when signs of drowsiness are detected.

      Scroll Up

    • 03 Collision Alerts and Prevention:

      Utilizing IoT technology and a WiFi microcontroller, DRIWAKE offers collision alerts by constantly analyzing the surroundings. In case of a potential collision, the system may autonomously control the vehicle's speed to prevent accidents.

      Scroll Up

    • 04 Smart Speed Control:

      DRIWAKE not only alerts the driver about trapped harmful gases but also takes proactive measures by dynamically adjusting the vehicle's speed based on the severity of the detected air quality issues. This feature ensures immediate response to potential health hazards.

      Scroll Up

    • 05 Real-time Location Tracking:

      The system continuously tracks the real-time location of the driver, providing accurate updates to both the truck owner and the company. This feature enhances fleet management capabilities and ensures better coordination.

      Scroll Up

    • 06 Emergency Response Integration:

      DRIWAKE is equipped to make emergency calls to nearby National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) centers and predefined emergency contacts. In the event of a critical situation, this functionality ensures swift response and assistance, further enhancing road safety.

    • CONTACT LIST INTEGRATION FOR ALERTS:

      THE DEVICE INCLUDES A FEATURE TO SAVE IMPORTANT CONTACTS RELATED TO THE DRIVER, SUCH AS FAMILY MEMBERS OR THE COMPANY'S MANAGEMENT. IN CASE OF DROWSINESS ALERTS OR COLLISION DETECTION, THE SYSTEM CAN NOTIFY THESE DESIGNATED CONTACTS. THIS FUNCTIONALITY ENSURES THAT RELEVANT PARTIES ARE INFORMED PROMPTLY, CONTRIBUTING TO A FASTER RESPONSE TO POTENTIAL EMERGENCIES.

    • IMMEDIATE ALERT FOR CLOSED EYES:

      WHEN THE EYE BLINK SENSOR DETECTS THAT THE DRIVER'S EYES HAVE BEEN CLOSED FOR AS LITTLE AS 1 SECOND, THE SYSTEM WILL TRIGGER AN IMMEDIATE ALARM, PROVIDING A REAL-TIME ALERT TO THE DRIVER.

    • SMART SPEED CONTROL

      SYSTEM HAS THE ABILITY TO SLOW DOWN THE VEHICLE'S SPEED. THIS FEATURE IS DESIGNED TO GRADUALLY AND SAFELY REDUCE THE TRUCK'S SPEED IN RESPONSE TO DRIVERS DROWSINESS.
